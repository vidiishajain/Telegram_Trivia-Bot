# Project Ideas

A grab-bag of fun things to build with this starter. Pick one that genuinely excites you —
that motivation is what gets you through the hard parts. Each one is just a starting point;
make it your own.

Once you've chosen, **don't jump to code** — fill in `docs/problem.md` and the goal in
`README.md`, then sketch the user stories, failure modes, and scenarios (see `CLAUDE.md` and
the worked `examples/*/docs/`). The method is the point; the idea is just the excuse.

**Capabilities legend:** 🖼️ image · 🎭 image→image · 🎬 video · 🔊 text-to-speech ·
🎙️ speech-to-text · 🧊 3D · 🔎 Perplexity search · 👁️ vision (image→text) · 🧠 LLM / structured
output · 🧮 embeddings + pgvector · 🗄️ database · 🤖 Telegram bot · 🌐 web app

> For models beyond the `text_to_image` / `text_to_speech` / `speech_to_text` helpers (video,
> 3D, image→image, …), call `media.generate("fal-ai/<model>", {...})` after reading that
> model's spec at `https://fal.ai/models/<model>/llms.txt`.

---

- **Birthday card maker** 🖼️🧠🎭 — answer a few questions about a friend (and optionally upload their photo to stylize) → a printable card you can download.
- **Food tracker bot** 🤖👁️🗄️🧮 — send a photo of a meal and it logs it; later ask "how much protein did I eat this week?"
- **Discipline collider** 🔎🧠 — find the latest papers in two unrelated fields and generate novel "what if they collide?" research ideas, with citations.
- **Avatar forge** 🎭 — upload a selfie and turn it into a Renaissance painting / muppet / pixel-art hero.
- **Receipts → CSV** 👁️🧠 — ingest a pile of receipt PDFs/photos, OCR and tag them, and produce a spending spreadsheet with totals.
- **Second brain** 🧮🗄️🧠 — dump your notes and links, embed them into pgvector, then ask questions of your own knowledge base.
- **Explain-like-I'm-___** 🧠🔊 — give a topic and an audience ("5-year-old", "medieval peasant", "CEO") and get an explanation read aloud.
- **Illustrated storybook** 🧠🖼️🗄️ — a child's name + a theme → a short story with one generated image per page, on a shareable web page.
- **3D trinket maker** 🧊 — describe an object and get back a downloadable 3D model.
- **Voice memo → tidy notes** 🎙️🧠 — drop in an audio recording → a clean transcript plus bullet-point summary and action items.
- **Finance ledger bot** 🤖🎙️🗄️🧠 — log spending by text or voice message → a running ledger → monthly reports and questions you can ask it.
- **Fridge → dinner** 👁️🧠🖼️ — a photo of your fridge → recipes you can actually make → a photo of the finished dish.
- **Album cover + fake tracklist** 🖼️🧠 — describe a vibe and get cover art, a band name, and a 10-song tracklist.
- **Daily digest → podcast** 🔎🧠🔊 — pick a few topics → a researched daily digest with sources → a short audio "podcast" version.
- **Text adventure with scene art** 🧠🖼️🗄️ — an LLM dungeon master that illustrates each turn and remembers your story.
- **"What is this?"** 👁️🔎 — point your camera at something → an identification plus three surprising facts (with sources).
- **Product photo studio** 🎭🧠 — a quick phone photo of a product → clean background + relit "studio" shots + ad copy.
- **Dub it** 🎙️🧠🔊 — upload a clip → transcribe → translate → re-voice it in another language.
- **Mood / habit journal bot** 🤖🗄️🧠🖼️ — a daily check-in → a weekly LLM summary plus an encouraging generated image.
- **Haiku from a photo** 👁️🧠🖼️ — a photo → a haiku about it → a matching minimalist illustration.
- **Idea → pitch kit** 🔎🧠🖼️ — a startup idea → a quick market scan + a one-pager + a logo + a hero image.
- **Flashcards from a doc** 🧠🗄️🌐 — upload your notes → auto-generated Q/A cards → a spaced-repetition web app.
- **Talking portrait / mini-explainer** 🧠🔊🎬 — a topic → a script → narration plus generated visuals → a short video.
